<p><p>Industrial lubricants can be categorized into three main types: conventional, synthetic, and bio-based/re-refined. Conventional lubricants are derived from mineral oils, offering good performance at a lower cost. Synthetic lubricants are chemically engineered for superior performance in extreme conditions, with longer service life and better stability. Bio-based/re-refined lubricants are derived from renewable sources or recycled materials, offering environmental benefits and sustainability. Each type of industrial lubricant has its own unique characteristics and advantages, catering to different industrial needs and preferences.</p></p>
