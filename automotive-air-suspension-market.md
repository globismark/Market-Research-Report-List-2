<p><strong>The Automotive Air Suspension Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Electronically Controlled</li><li>Electronically Controlled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive air suspension systems can be classified into two main types - Non-Electronically Controlled and Electronically Controlled. Non-Electronically Controlled systems rely on mechanical or manual adjustments to regulate the suspension, offering a simpler and more cost-effective solution. In contrast, Electronically Controlled systems utilize sensors and actuators to automatically adjust the suspension based on driving conditions, providing a more sophisticated and responsive ride experience. Both types of systems have their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific needs and preferences of the vehicle owner.</p></p>
