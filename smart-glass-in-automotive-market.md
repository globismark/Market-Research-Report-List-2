<p><p>Smart Glass in the automotive market refers to glass that can change its transparency based on external factors. There are two main types of smart glass in this market: Active Smart Windows and Passive Smart Windows. </p><p>Active Smart Windows use an external power source to regulate the transparency of the glass, allowing for on-demand adjustment. </p><p>Passive Smart Windows, on the other hand, rely on the material properties to automatically adjust transparency based on external factors such as light intensity or temperature. Both types offer advantages in terms of energy efficiency, visibility, and comfort for passengers.</p></p>
