<p><ul><li>Common Rail Fuel Injection Pump</li><li>Rotary Fuel Injection Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive fuel injection pump market is divided into two main types: Common Rail Fuel Injection Pump and Rotary Fuel Injection Pump. Common Rail Fuel Injection Pump uses a high-pressure fuel rail to supply fuel to individual injectors, allowing for precise fuel delivery. On the other hand, Rotary Fuel Injection Pump uses a rotating mechanism to generate pressure and deliver fuel to the injectors. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring efficient fuel delivery and optimal engine performance in vehicles.</p></p>
