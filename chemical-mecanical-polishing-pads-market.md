<p><p>Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) pads are used in the semiconductor industry for planarizing silicon wafers during the manufacturing process. There are two main types of CMP pads: Hard CMP pads and Soft CMP pads. Hard CMP pads are firm and have a high rigidity, resulting in faster material removal rates. Soft CMP pads, on the other hand, are more compliant and provide a smoother finish with less damage to the wafer surface. Both types of pads play a crucial role in achieving precise and uniform polishing results.</p></p>
