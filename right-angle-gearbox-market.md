<p><ul><li>Two-Way Right Angle Gearbox</li><li>Three-Way Right Angle Gearbox</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Right angle gearboxes are commonly classified into two main types: two-way right angle gearbox and three-way right angle gearbox. Two-way right angle gearboxes are designed to change the direction of rotational motion at a 90-degree angle, making them ideal for applications requiring a single directional change. On the other hand, three-way right angle gearboxes are capable of changing the direction of rotational motion in two different axes, allowing for more complex motion control applications. Both types of right angle gearboxes play crucial roles in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
