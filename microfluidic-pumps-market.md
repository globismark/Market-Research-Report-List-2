<p><p>Microfluidic pumps are essential components in microfluidic systems for precise and controlled fluid delivery. There are three main types of microfluidic pumps - syringe pump, peristaltic pump, and pressure pump. Syringe pumps operate by pushing or pulling a syringe plunger to control fluid flow. Peristaltic pumps utilize rollers to compress tubing and create fluid movement. Pressure pumps utilize compressed air or gas to drive fluid flow. Each type of pump offers unique advantages and is suited for different applications in the microfluidic industry.</p></p>
