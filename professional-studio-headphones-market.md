<p><strong>The Professional Studio Headphones Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Back</li><li>Semi-open Back</li><li>Fully-open Back</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Professional studio headphones are categorized into three main types based on the design of the ear cups: closed back, semi-open back, and fully open back. Closed-back headphones provide isolation from external noise and are ideal for tracking and recording in the studio. Semi-open-back headphones offer a balance between isolation and natural sound, making them suitable for mixing and editing. Fully open-back headphones allow for a more spacious sound but lack isolation, making them best for critical listening and mastering in a controlled environment.</p></p>
