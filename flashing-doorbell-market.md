<p><p>Flashing doorbells come in two main types: wired and wireless. Wired flashing doorbells require a direct connection to the electrical system of a home, providing a reliable and consistent signal for those with hearing impairments. On the other hand, wireless flashing doorbells operate on battery power and rely on radio frequency to communicate with the receiver, offering more flexibility in terms of installation and placement. Both types of flashing doorbells cater to individuals who may have difficulty hearing traditional doorbells.</p></p>
