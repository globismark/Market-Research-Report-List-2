<p><strong>The Automotive Smart Key Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Keyless Access (PKES)</li><li>Remote Keyless Access (RKES)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passive Keyless Entry System (PKES) allows the driver to unlock and start their vehicle without having to physically press any buttons on the key fob. The system automatically detects the key fob when it is within a certain range of the vehicle. On the other hand, Remote Keyless Entry System (RKES) requires the driver to press a button on the key fob to unlock or start the vehicle. Both systems offer convenience and enhanced security for vehicle owners.</p></p>
