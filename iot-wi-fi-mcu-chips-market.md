<p><p>Single Band IoT Wi-Fi MCU Chips are designed to operate on only one frequency band, typically 2.4 GHz. These chips are cost-effective and suitable for applications where basic connectivity is required. On the other hand, Dual Band IoT Wi-Fi MCU Chips can operate on both 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz frequency bands, offering faster and more reliable connections. Dual band chips are ideal for high-performance applications that require greater bandwidth and interference avoidance. Both types cater to different market needs based on performance, cost, and connectivity requirements.</p></p>
