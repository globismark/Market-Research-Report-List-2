<p><strong>The Automotive Automatic Clutch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Motor Automatic Clutch</li><li>Hydraulic Automatic Clutch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mechanical Motor Automatic Clutch uses mechanical components such as gears and springs to engage and disengage the clutch without the need for human intervention. This type of clutch is commonly found in older vehicles and requires manual operation. </p><p>Hydraulic Automatic Clutches, on the other hand, utilize hydraulic fluid to engage and disengage the clutch, providing a smoother and more efficient operation compared to mechanical clutches. Hydraulic automatic clutches are commonly used in modern vehicles for their reliability and ease of use.</p></p>
