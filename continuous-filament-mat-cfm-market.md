<p><p>Continuous Filament Mat (CFM) is a type of reinforcement material used in composite manufacturing. Powder Mats consist of continuous filaments coated with powdered resin, offering high strength and flexibility. Emulsion Mats, on the other hand, use continuous filaments coated with emulsion resin for improved bonding and durability. Both types of CFM are commonly used in industries such as automotive, construction, and marine for applications requiring superior strength and performance.</p></p>
