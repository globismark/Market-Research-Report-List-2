<p><ul><li>Powders</li><li>Solutions</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyvinyl chloride acetate resins are commonly found in two primary forms in the market - powders and solutions. Powders are finely ground particles of the resin that can be easily dispersed in various solvents for use in coatings, adhesives, and other applications. Solutions, on the other hand, are pre-dissolved resins that offer a convenient option for manufacturers looking to streamline their production processes. Both types provide versatility and flexibility in formulation, making them highly sought after in the chemical industry.</p></p>
