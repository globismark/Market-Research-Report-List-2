<p><p>Car air suspension systems come in various types, including convoluted, sleeve, and others. Convoluted air springs consist of a single rubber bellows, providing a smooth and comfortable ride. Sleeve air springs, on the other hand, feature a sleeve-shaped design that offers adjustable ride height and load support. Other types of air suspension systems may include multi-bellows designs or electronically controlled systems for advanced performance and customization options. Each type offers unique benefits and is designed to meet specific requirements for vehicle suspension.</p></p>
