<p><p>Pipe insulation materials market includes various types such as fiberglass, mineral wool, elastomeric foam, polyurethane foam, and others. Fiberglass insulation is the most common type, known for its thermal efficiency and fire-resistant properties. Mineral wool offers high thermal performance and is resistant to moisture. Elastomeric foam is flexible and durable, making it ideal for chilled water applications. Polyurethane foam provides excellent insulation and moisture resistance. Other materials like cellular glass and polystyrene are also used in pipe insulation for specific applications.</p></p>
