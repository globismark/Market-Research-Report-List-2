<p><p>Linear rotary actuators are mechanical devices used to convert rotary motion into linear motion or vice versa. They are available in three main types - pneumatic, hydraulic, and electric. Pneumatic actuators use compressed air to generate force, hydraulic actuators use pressurized fluid, typically oil, and electric actuators use an electric motor to produce linear motion. Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages, including cost, speed, precision, and reliability, making them suitable for different applications in industries such as manufacturing, automation, and robotics.</p></p>
