<p><ul><li>Single coil</li><li>Multi-coil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Ignition Module market can be categorized into two main types: single coil and multi-coil systems. Single coil systems utilize a single ignition coil to generate spark for all cylinders in the engine, while multi-coil systems have individual ignition coils for each cylinder. Single coil systems are simpler and less expensive, but multi-coil systems provide more precise ignition timing and better performance. Both types have their own advantages and are used in different applications depending on the specific requirements of the engine.</p></p>
