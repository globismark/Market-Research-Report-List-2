<p><p>Rivet guns are tools used for fastening metal pieces together. They are available in three main types: manual rivet guns, pneumatic rivet guns, and electric rivet guns. Manual rivet guns require physical force to operate, making them suitable for small-scale projects. Pneumatic rivet guns use compressed air for power, offering faster and more efficient operation, ideal for industrial applications. Electric rivet guns are powered by electricity, providing consistent performance and precision, commonly used in manufacturing and assembly processes.</p></p>
