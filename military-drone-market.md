<p><p>The military drone market is segmented into two main types based on their design and operation: Fixed Wing and Rotary Wing drones. Fixed Wing drones are similar to traditional airplanes, with a fixed wing that provides lift and propulsion to the vehicle. They are known for their longer flight times and higher speeds, making them suitable for long-range reconnaissance and surveillance missions. On the other hand, Rotary Wing drones, also known as quadcopters or multirotors, are more maneuverable and versatile, making them ideal for short-range missions such as close-quarters surveillance or small-scale strikes.</p></p>
