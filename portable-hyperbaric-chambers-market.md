<p><p>Portable Hyperbaric Chambers are available in two main types: Horizontal Chambers and Vertical Chambers. Horizontal Chambers are designed for the patient to lie down flat, with the chamber surrounding their entire body. This type of chamber provides equal pressure across the body and is suitable for patients who prefer to lay down during treatment. Vertical Chambers, on the other hand, are designed for the patient to sit or stand upright during treatment. This type of chamber is more compact and may be preferred by patients who have limited space.</p></p>
