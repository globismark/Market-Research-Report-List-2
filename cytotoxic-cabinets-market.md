<p><p>Cytotoxic cabinets are specialized cabinets used for handling hazardous materials like cytotoxic drugs. There are two main types of cytotoxic cabinets - Class II and Class III. Class II cabinets provide protection for the user, product, and environment through the use of HEPA filtered air. Class III cabinets offer the highest level of protection, with a totally enclosed, gas-tight workspace to prevent any leakage of harmful substances. Both types of cabinets are essential for ensuring safe handling of cytotoxic materials in healthcare settings.</p></p>
