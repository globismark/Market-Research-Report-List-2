<p><p>Nasopharyngeal swabs are commonly made from either polyethylene (PE) or polypropylene (PP) materials. PE swabs are known for their flexibility and softness, making them ideal for sensitive nasal passages. On the other hand, PP swabs are more rigid and durable, suitable for more vigorous swabbing procedures. Both types of swabs are widely used in medical settings for collecting samples for diagnostic testing. The choice between PE and PP swabs depends on the specific needs of the healthcare provider and patient.</p></p>
