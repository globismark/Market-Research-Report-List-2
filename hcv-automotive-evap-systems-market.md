<p><p>The HCV Automotive EVAP Systems market consists of various types of components including the Fuel Tank, Gas Cap, Liquid Vapor Separator, Control Valve, Sensor, and Hoses. The Fuel Tank stores the fuel vapor, while the Gas Cap seals the tank. The Liquid Vapor Separator separates liquid fuel from vapor. The Control Valve regulates the flow of vapor, while the Sensor detects leaks or malfunctions. Hoses connect these components to ensure proper functioning of the EVAP system in heavy commercial vehicles.</p></p>
