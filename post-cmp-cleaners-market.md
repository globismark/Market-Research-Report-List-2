<p><ul><li>Acidic Post CMP Cleaners</li><li>Alkaline Post CMP Cleaners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Post CMP cleaners are used in the semiconductor industry to remove residue left over from chemical mechanical polishing processes. Acidic post CMP cleaners are formulated with acidic components to dissolve and remove metal and oxide residues. Alkaline post CMP cleaners, on the other hand, are formulated with alkaline components to remove organic and ionic residues. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring clean and smooth surfaces for semiconductor devices, each catering to specific cleaning needs in the industry.</p></p>
