<p><p>The Super Engineering Plastic market is divided into two main types: Injection Molding Grade and Extrusion Grade. Injection Molding Grade plastics are specifically designed for use in injection molding processes, where the material is melted and injected into a mold to create complex shapes. Extrusion Grade plastics, on the other hand, are formulated for use in extrusion processes, where the material is forced through a die to create long, continuous shapes. Both types of plastics offer high performance and durability for a wide range of applications.</p></p>
