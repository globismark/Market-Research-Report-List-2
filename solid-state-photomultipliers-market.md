<p><p>Solid-State Photomultipliers are available in two main market types: Monocoque Type and Array Type. Monocoque Type photomultipliers consist of a single module that integrates all necessary components into a compact design, making them ideal for applications requiring low power consumption and space-saving solutions. On the other hand, Array Type photomultipliers consist of multiple modules arranged in an array configuration, providing higher sensitivity and resolution for applications that demand high performance. Both types offer unique advantages and cater to different market needs in the field of photon detection technology.</p></p>
