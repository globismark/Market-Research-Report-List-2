<p><ul><li>Single Row Nanominiature Connectors</li><li>Dual Row Nanominiature Connectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nanominiature connectors are categorized into single row and dual row types based on their designs. Single row nanominiature connectors feature a single row of contact pins, making them suitable for applications requiring a compact size and lightweight design. On the other hand, dual row nanominiature connectors consist of two parallel rows of contact pins, offering higher contact density and increased signal transmission capabilities. Both types cater to different requirements in the electronics industry, providing flexibility in connectivity solutions.</p></p>
