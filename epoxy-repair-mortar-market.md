<p><ul><li>Two-Component Epoxy Mortar</li><li>Three-Component Epoxy Mortar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Epoxy repair mortar is categorized into two main types: two-component epoxy mortar and three-component epoxy mortar. Two-component epoxy mortar consists of a resin and hardener that are mixed together before application, offering a convenient and user-friendly solution for repairing damaged surfaces. On the other hand, three-component epoxy mortar includes an additional component, typically an aggregate or filler, which enhances the strength and durability of the repaired area. Both types of epoxy mortar are widely used in construction and infrastructure repair projects.</p></p>
