<p><p>Check valves are devices used to prevent backflow in piping systems. The market for check valves can be divided into different types based on their design and function. Swing check valves are the most common type, with a swinging disc that opens and closes to allow or stop flow. Lift check valves use a piston or ball to lift up and allow flow in one direction only. Other types of check valves include ball check valves, dual-plate check valves, and stop-check valves, each offering unique features for specific applications.</p></p>
