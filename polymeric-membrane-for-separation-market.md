<p><p>Polymeric membrane for separation market types include hollow fiber, spiral wound, and others. Hollow fiber membranes consist of many small tubes that allow for efficient separation of materials. Spiral wound membranes are made of flat sheets wound around a central tube, providing a large surface area for separation. Other types may include tubular, capillary, or flat sheet membranes. Each type offers unique advantages and is used in various industries for tasks such as water purification, gas separation, and pharmaceutical processing.</p></p>
