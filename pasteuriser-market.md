<p><ul><li>Plate Heat Exchangers</li><li>Shell or Tube Heat Exchangers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pasteuriser Market is segmented into two types based on heat exchangers: Plate Heat Exchangers and Shell or Tube Heat Exchangers. Plate Heat Exchangers use metal plates to transfer heat between two fluids, providing efficient heating or cooling. On the other hand, Shell or Tube Heat Exchangers use tubes or shells to transfer heat, suitable for high-pressure applications. Both types offer efficient heat transfer and ensure proper pasteurization of liquids, catering to various industries including food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and dairy.</p></p>
