<p><p>Carrier tape is a packaging technology used in the electronics industry to hold and transport small electronic components. There are two main types of carrier tapes: paper core carrier tape and plastic core carrier tape. Paper core carrier tape is made with a paper material as its core, offering eco-friendly packaging solution. On the other hand, plastic core carrier tape utilizes a plastic material as its core, providing a more durable and robust packaging option. Both types of carrier tapes serve the same purpose of protecting and transporting electronic components during manufacturing and assembly processes.</p></p>
