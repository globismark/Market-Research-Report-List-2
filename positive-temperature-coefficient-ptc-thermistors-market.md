<p><p>Positive Temperature Coefficient (PTC) Thermistors have two main market types - Ceramic PTC (CPTC) and Polymer PTC (PPTC). CPTC thermistors are made of ceramic materials and provide a relatively stable resistance over a wide temperature range. On the other hand, PPTC thermistors are made of polymer materials and exhibit a sharp increase in resistance when the temperature exceeds a certain threshold. Both market types are used in various applications such as automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial equipment for temperature sensing and overcurrent protection.</p></p>
