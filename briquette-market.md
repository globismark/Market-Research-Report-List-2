<p><p>Briquette markets are classified into three main types: Solid Cylindrical Type, RUF Type, and Pini-Kay Type. Solid Cylindrical Type briquettes are compact and dense, making them ideal for long burning times. RUF Type briquettes are rectangular with pre-cut grooves, offering easy handling and consistent burning. Pini-Kay Type briquettes are dense and have a hole in the middle for better airflow, resulting in efficient burning. Each type caters to different preferences and needs in the market for solid fuel alternatives.</p></p>
