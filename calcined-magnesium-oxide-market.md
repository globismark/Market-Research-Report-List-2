<p><p>Calcined Magnesium Oxide is produced in two main types - Caustic Calcined (Light Burned) and Heavy Burned. Caustic Calcined Magnesium Oxide is processed at lower temperatures, resulting in a lighter color and higher reactivity. It is commonly used in industries like agriculture and environmental protection. On the other hand, Heavy Burned Magnesium Oxide is produced at higher temperatures, resulting in a darker color and lower reactivity. It is often used in industries like refractories and construction.</p></p>
