<p><ul><li>Type III</li><li>Type IV</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Composite pressure vessels are categorized into Type III, Type IV, and Others. Type III vessels are made of a metal liner, wrapped with a composite material, providing good durability and pressure containment. Type IV vessels have a fully non-metallic construction, offering lightweight and high-pressure storage capabilities. The Others category includes vessels with unique designs or materials that do not fit into Type III or Type IV classifications. These different types cater to various industries and applications based on their specific performance characteristics and requirements.</p></p>
