<p><p>Infant resuscitators are devices used to provide artificial ventilation to newborn babies who are experiencing breathing difficulties. There are two main types of infant resuscitators in the market - self-inflating resuscitators and flow-inflating resuscitators.</p><p>Self-inflating resuscitators have a bag that self-inflates when squeezed, allowing for easy delivery of breaths to the baby. On the other hand, flow-inflating resuscitators require a consistent flow of oxygen to inflate the bag, providing more control over the delivered breaths. Both types are essential tools in neonatal care for assisting with newborn resuscitation.</p></p>
