<p><p>Gravity filters are commonly used in industries for solid-liquid separation. There are two main types of gravity filters in the market - Vertical Gravity Separators and Horizontal Gravity Separators. Vertical gravity separators use the force of gravity to separate solid particles from liquids by passing the mixture through a series of vertical chambers. Horizontal gravity separators operate in a similar way but use horizontal chambers for the separation process. Both types of gravity filters are effective in removing impurities from various liquids in industrial applications.</p></p>
