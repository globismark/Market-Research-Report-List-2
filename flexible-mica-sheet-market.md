<p><p>Flexible Mica Sheets are available in two types - Phlogopite Mica and Muscovite Mica. Phlogopite Mica is known for its resistance to high temperatures and flexibility, making it suitable for use in electrical insulation, thermal insulation, and furnace lining. On the other hand, Muscovite Mica is characterized by its transparency, flexibility, and excellent dielectric strength, making it suitable for applications such as electronic devices, appliances, and cosmetics. Both types of Mica Sheets offer unique properties and serve a variety of industrial purposes.</p></p>
