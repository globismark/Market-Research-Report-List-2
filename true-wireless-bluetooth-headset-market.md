<p><p>The True Wireless Bluetooth Headset market can be categorized into three main types based on the design of the headphones. </p><p>1. Full Cup (Circumaural) headsets cover the entire ear, providing a comfortable fit and good noise isolation. </p><p>2. On-Ear (Supraaural) headsets rest on the outer ear, offering a more lightweight design and less noise isolation compared to full cup headphones. </p><p>3. In-Ear (Intraaural) headsets fit directly into the ear canal, offering a compact design and excellent noise isolation. Each type caters to different preferences and needs of consumers in the market.</p></p>
