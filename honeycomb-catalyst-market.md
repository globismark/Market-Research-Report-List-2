<p><p>Honeycomb catalyst is a type of catalyst used in various industrial processes such as automotive, petrochemical, and chemical manufacturing. It consists of a honeycomb-like structure made of a material such as metal or ceramic, which is coated with catalytic elements like platinum, palladium, or rhodium. Honeycomb catalysts are known for their high efficiency in converting harmful pollutants into less harmful substances, making them essential in reducing emissions and improving air quality.</p><p>The global honeycomb catalyst market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.3% during the forecast period.
