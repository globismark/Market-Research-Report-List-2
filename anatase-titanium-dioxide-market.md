<p><p>Anatase Titanium Dioxide Market Types include the Sulfate Process and Chloride Process. The Sulfate Process involves dissolving ilmenite ore in sulfuric acid to produce titanium dioxide, while the Chloride Process uses chlorine to chemically extract titanium dioxide from ilmenite or rutile. The Sulfate Process is more commonly used due to its lower production costs, but the Chloride Process is known for producing higher purity titanium dioxide. Both processes cater to different market demands and applications within the titanium dioxide industry.</p></p>
