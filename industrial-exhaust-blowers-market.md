<p><p>Industrial Exhaust Blowers are categorized into three main types - Centrifugal Blower, Axial Blower, and Others. Centrifugal blowers work by increasing the speed and pressure of air through a rotating impeller. Axial blowers move air in a straight line, parallel to the impeller shaft. Other types of blowers include regenerative blowers, crossflow blowers, and tangential or radial blowers. Each type of blower is suitable for different industrial applications based on factors like air flow requirements, pressure levels, and space constraints.</p></p>
