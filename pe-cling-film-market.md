<p><p>LDPE, LLDPE, and HDPE are different types of polyethylene plastics used in the PE Cling Film market. LDPE, or Low-Density Polyethylene, offers excellent flexibility and transparency, making it ideal for wrapping food items. LLDPE, or Linear Low-Density Polyethylene, provides higher strength and puncture resistance, commonly used for heavier and more durable packaging. HDPE, or High-Density Polyethylene, is known for its stiffness and toughness, making it suitable for industrial and heavy-duty applications. Each type of PE plastic caters to specific needs in the market, offering a range of options for manufacturers and consumers.</p></p>
