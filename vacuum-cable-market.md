<p><p>Vacuum cable market is segmented into three main types: High Vacuum, Ultra-High Vacuum (UHV), and Extreme High Vacuum (XHV). High Vacuum cables are designed for applications that require a pressure range of 1x10^-3 to 1x10^-7 torr. UHV cables are suitable for environments with pressures down to 1x10^-9 torr, while XHV cables are intended for extreme conditions with pressures as low as 1x10^-12 torr. These different types cater to varying levels of vacuum requirements in industries such as semiconductor manufacturing, research labs, and space exploration.</p></p>
