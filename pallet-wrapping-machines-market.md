<p><ul><li>Semi-Automatic</li><li>Fully Automatic</li><li>Manual</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pallet wrapping machines come in three main types: semi-automatic, fully automatic, and manual. Semi-automatic machines require some level of human intervention to operate, such as attaching the film to the pallet before starting the wrapping process. Fully automatic machines can wrap pallets entirely on their own without human assistance. Manual machines require the operator to manually wrap the pallet using a hand-operated crank or lever. Each type of machine offers different levels of efficiency and convenience for businesses in need of pallet wrapping solutions.</p></p>
