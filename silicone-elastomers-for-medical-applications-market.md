<p><p>Silicone elastomers are widely used in medical applications due to their biocompatibility, chemical resistance, and stability over a wide range of temperatures. The market for silicone elastomers can be divided into three main types: High Temperature Vulcanized (HTV), Room Temperature Vulcanized (RTV), and Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR). HTV elastomers are cured at high temperatures to achieve their final properties, while RTV elastomers cure at room temperature. LSR elastomers are processed using liquid injection molding, making them highly suitable for intricate medical device components.</p></p>
