<p><p>A Content Delivery Network (CDN) is a system of distributed servers that work together to deliver web content, such as images, videos, and other static assets, to users based on their geographic locations. CDN improves the speed and performance of websites by caching content closer to users, reducing latency and improving overall user experience.</p><p>The Content Delivery Network Market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for faster and more reliable content delivery across the globe. With the rise of online content consumption, businesses are increasingly relying on CDNs to ensure seamless delivery of their digital assets to users, regardless of their location or device. The market is also driven by the growing adoption of cloud-based services and the proliferation of high-quality multimedia content.</p><p>Moreover, the COVID-19 pandemic has further accelerated the adoption of CDN services as more people are working remotely and consuming online content. The market is also seeing the emergence of new trends such as edge computing, which brings data processing closer to the end-users, further boosting the demand for CDN services.</p><p>Overall, the Content Delivery Network Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.4% during the forecast period, driven by the increasing need for efficient content delivery in today's digital age.</p></p>
