<p><p>Full height turnstiles are security gates that restrict access to a specific area by only allowing one person to pass through at a time. They come in two main types: single lane and dual lane. Single lane turnstiles are designed for areas with lower traffic volume, allowing for one person to pass through at a time. Dual lane turnstiles, on the other hand, are ideal for areas with higher traffic volume, as they allow for two people to pass through simultaneously, increasing efficiency and reducing wait times.</p></p>
