<p><strong>The Optical Line Protection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>1+1 Optical Line Protection</li><li>1:1 Optical Line Protection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Line Protection refers to the technique used in optical communication networks to ensure uninterrupted data transmission. The market includes two main types: 1+1 Optical Line Protection and 1:1 Optical Line Protection. In 1+1 protection, two fiber paths are used, with data transmitting simultaneously. If one path fails, the system automatically switches to the other path, ensuring continuous data flow. In 1:1 protection, a primary and backup fiber path are designated, with data transmitting only on the primary path. If the primary path fails, the system switches to the backup path to maintain continuity.</p></p>
