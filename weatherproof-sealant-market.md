<p><strong>The Weatherproof Sealant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-borne</li><li>Solvent-borne</li><li>Reactive</li><li>Hot-melts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Weatherproof sealants are available in various types such as water-borne, solvent-borne, reactive, and hot-melts. Water-borne sealants are environmentally friendly, easy to clean and have low VOC content. Solvent-borne sealants are durable and offer good adhesion to different surfaces. Reactive sealants cure through a chemical reaction, offering high strength and durability. Hot-melts are applied in a molten state and solidify upon cooling, providing quick and strong bonds. Each type of sealant has its own advantages and is suitable for specific applications.</p></p>
