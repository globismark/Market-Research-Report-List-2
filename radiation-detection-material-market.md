<p><ul><li>Semiconductors</li><li>Scintillators</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiation detection materials can be categorized into three main types: Semiconductors, Scintillators, and Others. Semiconductors are materials that can detect radiation by generating an electrical signal in response to interaction with radiation. Scintillators are materials that emit light when exposed to radiation, which can then be detected. The category of Others includes various materials such as gas detectors, solid-state detectors, and chemical detectors that can also be used for radiation detection purposes. Each type of material has its own unique properties and applications in the field of radiation detection.</p></p>
