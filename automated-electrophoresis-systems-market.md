<p><strong>The Automated Electrophoresis Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fully Automated</li><li>Semi Automated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated electrophoresis systems are classified into two main types: Fully Automated and Semi Automated. Fully Automated systems do not require any manual intervention and perform all steps of electrophoresis automatically, from sample loading to analysis. In contrast, Semi Automated systems still require some manual steps, such as sample loading or data interpretation, but automate the majority of the electrophoresis process. Both types offer benefits in terms of accuracy, efficiency, and reproducibility in various applications like genomics, proteomics, and clinical diagnostics.</p></p>
