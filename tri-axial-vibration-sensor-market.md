<p><p>Tri-axial vibration sensor market includes accelerometers and velocity sensors. Accelerometers measure the acceleration of an object in three axes, providing data on the rate of change of velocity. This data is crucial in monitoring vibrations in various applications such as industrial machinery and automotive vehicles. Velocity sensors, on the other hand, measure the velocity of an object in three axes, offering insights into the speed and direction of vibrations. Both types of sensors play a key role in detecting and analyzing vibrations in different industries.</p></p>
