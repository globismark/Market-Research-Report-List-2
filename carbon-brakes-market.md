<p><p>Carbon brakes market can be categorized into two types based on the materials used: Carbon-Ceramic Composite Materials and Carbon-Carbon Composite Materials. Carbon-Ceramic Composite Materials are made by reinforcing carbon fibers with ceramic materials, providing high thermal resistance and durability. On the other hand, Carbon-Carbon Composite Materials are manufactured by bonding carbon fibers together with a carbon matrix, offering superior performance in high-temperature environments. Both types of brakes are widely used in aerospace and automotive industries for their lightweight, high strength, and heat resistance properties.</p></p>
