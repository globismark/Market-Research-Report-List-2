<p><ul><li>Piezoresistive Sensors</li><li>PN Junction Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor pressure sensors can be divided into two main types: piezoresistive sensors and PN junction sensors. Piezoresistive sensors function by changing the resistance of the material in response to pressure, while PN junction sensors utilize the electrical properties of a semiconductor material to measure pressure changes. Both types offer high accuracy and sensitivity in pressure measurement applications. Piezoresistive sensors are commonly used in automotive and industrial applications, while PN junction sensors are more suitable for harsh environments due to their rugged design.</p></p>
