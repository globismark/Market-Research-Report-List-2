<p><p>Parabolic antennas are commonly used for long-distance communication and broadcast due to their high gain and directivity. The Electronically Controlled Antenna (ESA) market involves antennas that can electronically change their beam direction, making them more flexible and efficient. On the other hand, the Mechanically Controlled Antenna (MSA) market consists of antennas that require physical movement to change their beam direction. ESAs are becoming more popular due to their ability to quickly adapt to changing communication needs, while MSAs are still widely used in many applications.</p></p>
