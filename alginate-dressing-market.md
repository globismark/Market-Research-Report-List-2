<p><p>Alginate dressings are advanced wound care products that are made from natural seaweed and are designed to promote healing in moderate to heavily exuding wounds. They come in various types, including flake dressings and strip dressings. Flake dressings are small pieces of alginate that can be applied to irregularly shaped wounds, while strip dressings are long, narrow pieces that are ideal for packing deep wounds. Both types of alginate dressings provide a moist healing environment, absorb excess exudate, and can be easily removed without causing trauma to the wound bed.</p></p>
