<p><p>Flat wire belts can be classified into two main market types based on their design and construction: flexible and rigid. Flexible flat wire belts are designed to easily conform to various shapes and curves, making them ideal for applications where the belt needs to navigate corners or bends. On the other hand, rigid flat wire belts are built with a stiffer construction, providing stability and support for heavy loads or high-speed applications. Both types offer unique advantages and are chosen based on specific operational requirements.</p></p>
