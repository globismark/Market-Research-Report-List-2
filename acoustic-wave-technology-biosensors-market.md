<p><p>Acoustic wave technology biosensors, such as Quartz Crystal Microbalance (QCM) sensors and Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) sensors, are used in various industries for detecting and measuring chemical and biological substances. QCM sensors utilize the resonance frequency of a quartz crystal to measure mass changes on the crystal surface, while SAW sensors use acoustic waves to detect changes in the properties of the sensor surface. Both types of sensors offer high sensitivity, fast response times, and real-time monitoring capabilities, making them valuable tools in research and industry applications.</p></p>
