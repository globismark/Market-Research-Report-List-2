<p><p>RF Surface Acoustic Wave Filters are commonly classified into two main types: Transversal Filters and Resonator Filters. Transversal Filters utilize a cascade of delay lines to achieve filtering of RF signals, while Resonator Filters rely on acoustic wave resonators to select and transmit certain frequencies.Both types of filters play a crucial role in applications requiring precise frequency selection and signal processing, such as telecommunications, radar systems, and wireless communication devices. The market for these filters continues to grow due to the increasing demand for high-performance RF signal filtering solutions.</p></p>
