<p><ul><li>Microfluidic Chip Digital PCR</li><li>Droplet Digital PCR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital PCR platforms are revolutionizing the field of genetic analysis by offering more precise and sensitive quantification of DNA samples. Two popular types of digital PCR platforms are Microfluidic Chip Digital PCR and Droplet Digital PCR. Microfluidic Chip Digital PCR utilizes microfluidic technology to partition DNA samples into individual reactions, allowing for high-throughput analysis. Droplet Digital PCR, on the other hand, partitions DNA samples into droplets for analysis, providing an alternative approach to achieving accurate quantification of DNA targets. Both platforms offer unique advantages in terms of sensitivity and scalability in genetic analysis.</p></p>
