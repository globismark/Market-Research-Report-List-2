<p><strong>The Waterjet Cutting Machinery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressurized Water</li><li>Abrasive Mixture</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pressurized Water market refers to waterjet cutting machines that use only high-pressure water to cut materials. This type of machinery is suitable for cutting soft materials like rubber, foam, and thin plastics. Abrasive Mixture market involves waterjet cutting machines that combine high-pressure water with abrasive materials like garnet or aluminum oxide to cut harder materials such as metals, stone, and glass. These machines are ideal for industries requiring precision cutting of dense and thick materials. Both types of markets cater to different cutting requirements based on material hardness and thickness.</p></p>
