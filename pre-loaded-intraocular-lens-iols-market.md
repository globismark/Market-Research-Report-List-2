<p><p>Preloaded intraocular lens (IOLs) come in different types to cater to a variety of patient needs. Monofocal IOLs provide fixed focus at one distance, often requiring glasses for near or distance vision. Multifocal IOLs offer multiple focal points, reducing the need for glasses after surgery. Toric IOLs correct astigmatism in addition to near or distance vision. Other types of preloaded IOLs may include extended depth of focus or accommodating lenses, providing unique benefits for specific patient preferences and visual requirements.</p></p>
