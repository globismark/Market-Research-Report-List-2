<p><p>Plastic Optical Fiber market consists of two main types: PMMA (Polymer Polymethylmethacrylate) and Perfluorinated Polymers. PMMA fibers are the most commonly used due to their low cost and ease of use, making them ideal for short-range communication applications. On the other hand, Perfluorinated Polymers offer superior optical performance and are better suited for long-range data transmission applications. Both types of fibers have their own advantages and are used in various industries depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
