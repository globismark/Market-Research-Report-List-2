<p><p>Brake oil market is categorized into three main types based on their composition: castor oil-based, glycol-based, and silicone-based. Castor oil-based brake oil is known for its high stability and resistance to oxidation. Glycol-based brake oil is popular for its wide temperature range and low viscosity. Silicone-based brake oil is favored for its resistance to water absorption and compatibility with rubber components. Each type caters to different needs and preferences of consumers in the automotive industry.</p></p>
