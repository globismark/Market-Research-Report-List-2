<p><strong>The Electrostatic Wafer Chuck Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electrostatic Wafer Chuck market includes two main types: Coulomb Type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type. Coulomb Type wafer chucks rely on the attraction between charges to hold the wafer in place, while JR Type chucks use a combination of Coulomb forces and Van der Waals forces for improved stability. Coulomb Type chucks are simpler and more cost-effective, while JR Type chucks offer better performance and control. Both types are used in semiconductor manufacturing for holding wafers during processing.</p></p>
