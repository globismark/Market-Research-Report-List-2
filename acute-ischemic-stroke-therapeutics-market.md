<p><p>Acute Ischemic Stroke Therapeutics market consists of various types of treatments aimed at managing and improving outcomes for patients with ischemic stroke. Thrombolytic Therapeutics involve medications that dissolve blood clots to restore blood flow to the brain. Antihypertensive Therapeutics help in controlling high blood pressure, a major risk factor for stroke. Antiplatelet Therapeutics prevent blood clots from forming. Anticoagulants help in reducing the risk of clot formation. These different types of therapeutics play a crucial role in the management of acute ischemic stroke.</p></p>
