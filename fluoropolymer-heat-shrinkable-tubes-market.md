<p><p>Fluoropolymer Heat Shrinkable Tubes are classified into various types based on their chemical composition and properties. PFA (Perfluoroalkoxy) tubes offer excellent high temperature resistance and chemical inertness. FEP (Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene) tubes provide good electrical insulation and low friction. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) tubes are known for their non-stick properties and high chemical resistance. PVDF (Polyvinylidene Fluoride) tubes have good mechanical strength and abrasion resistance. Other types of tubes in the market may include ETFE (Ethylene Tetrafluoroethylene) or ECTFE (Ethylene Chlorotrifluoroethylene) tubes with unique properties suited for specific applications.</p></p>
