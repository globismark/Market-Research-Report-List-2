<p><strong>The Run-Out Gauge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Run-Out</li><li>Axial Run-Out</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Run-Out Gauge market consists of two main types: Radial Run-Out and Axial Run-Out. Radial Run-Out refers to the measurement of the imperfections in a circular surface, while Axial Run-Out measures the deviation of a surface from its central axis. Radial Run-Out is more commonly used in industries such as automotive and aerospace, where precise roundness is crucial. Axial Run-Out, on the other hand, is often seen in applications where straightness and alignment are important, such as in machinery and equipment manufacturing. Both types of Run-Out Gauge play a vital role in ensuring the quality and accuracy of machined parts and components.</p></p>
