<p><p>Flexible substrates for 5G market can be categorized into three main types: PI (polyimide), LCP (liquid crystal polymer), and others. PI substrates offer high temperature resistance and flexibility, making them suitable for high-frequency applications in 5G technology. LCP substrates provide excellent electrical properties and dimensional stability, making them ideal for high-speed data transmission. Other types of flexible substrates for 5G may include materials such as PET or PEN, each offering unique features that cater to different requirements in the fast-growing 5G market.</p></p>
