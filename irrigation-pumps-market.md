<p><strong>The Irrigation Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Submersible</li><li>Non-submersible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Irrigation pumps can be classified as either submersible or non-submersible based on their usage and installation. Submersible pumps are designed to be fully immersed in water, making them ideal for pumping water from deep underground sources. Non-submersible pumps, on the other hand, are installed above the water surface and are commonly used for shallow water applications. Both types of pumps serve the purpose of providing water for irrigation, but their design and installation differ according to the specific needs of the user.</p></p>
