<p><p>Electroless copper plating is commonly used in the production of multi-layered circuit boards. Horizontal electroless copper plating involves the deposition of a uniform layer of copper onto a flat surface, while vertical electroless copper plating is used to coat the walls of small holes or vias in the circuit board. Both processes are essential for creating reliable connections between different layers of the board, ensuring optimal performance and durability in electronic devices.</p></p>
