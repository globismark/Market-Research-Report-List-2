<p><p>Biochemical sensors are classified into different types based on their operating principles. Electrochemical sensors detect chemical compounds based on their electrical properties, while thermal sensors measure changes in temperature caused by biochemical reactions. Piezoelectric sensors detect bio-molecules through mechanical vibrations, and optical sensors use light to detect biochemical reactions. Each type of sensor has its own unique advantages and applications, making them valuable tools in various industries such as healthcare, environmental monitoring, and food safety.</p></p>
