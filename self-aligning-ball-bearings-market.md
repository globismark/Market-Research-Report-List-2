<p><p>Self-aligning ball bearings are designed to accommodate misalignment and shaft deflections. They consist of two rows of balls that run in an inner ring with two raceways and an outer ring with a single raceway. This design allows the bearing to self-align and compensate for any angular misalignment between the shaft and the housing. Self-aligning ball bearings are commonly used in applications where shaft deflections are common, such as in agricultural equipment, construction machinery, and industrial fans.</p><p>The global self-aligning ball bearings market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.9% during the forecast period.
