<p><strong>The Automotive Carbon Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resin Transfer Molding</li><li>Vacuum Infusion Processing</li><li>Injection Molding</li><li>Compression Molding</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive carbon fiber market types include Resin Transfer Molding (RTM), Vacuum Infusion Processing, Injection Molding, Compression Molding, and other methods. RTM involves injecting resin into a closed mold with pre-placed fibers. Vacuum Infusion Processing uses vacuum pressure to infuse resin into the carbon fiber. Injection Molding melts plastic pellets and injects them into a mold. Compression Molding compresses carbon fiber and resin in a heated mold. Other methods may include filament winding, prepreg molding, and sheet molding compounds. Each method offers unique benefits and applications in the automotive industry.</p></p>
