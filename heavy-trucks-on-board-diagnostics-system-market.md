<p><p>The Heavy Trucks On-board Diagnostics System Market includes two types of diagnostic tools: Hand-Held Scan Tools and Mobile Device-Based Tools. Hand-Held Scan Tools are portable devices that can be connected to a truck's diagnostic port to read and analyze data related to the vehicle's performance. On the other hand, Mobile Device-Based Tools are software applications installed on smartphones or tablets that can communicate with the truck's onboard computer system to diagnose and troubleshoot issues. Both types of tools help truck owners and operators efficiently maintain and repair their vehicles.</p></p>
