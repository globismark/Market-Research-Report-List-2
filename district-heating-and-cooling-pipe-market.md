<p><p>District heating and cooling pipe market consists of two main types: heating pipes and cooling pipes. Heating pipes are designed to transport hot water or steam from a centralized heating plant to residential or commercial buildings for space heating. Cooling pipes, on the other hand, deliver chilled water or refrigerant to provide air conditioning to buildings. Both types of pipes are essential components of district heating and cooling systems, helping to efficiently distribute energy for thermal comfort in urban areas.</p></p>
