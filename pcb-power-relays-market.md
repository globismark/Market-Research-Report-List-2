<p><p>PCB Power Relays are classified into four main types based on their power handling capacities: Micro Power Relays, Low Power Relays, Medium Power Relays, and High Power Relays. Micro Power Relays are designed for handling very low power levels, whereas Low Power Relays are suitable for applications requiring slightly higher power capacities. Medium Power Relays offer a moderate power handling capacity, while High Power Relays are capable of handling the highest power levels among the four types. Each type caters to different applications based on their power requirements.</p></p>
