<p><strong>The Composite Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parallel Composite Fibres</li><li>Cortical Composite Fibres</li><li>Island Composite Fibres</li><li>Dispersed Primary Fibres</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Composite fiber market types include parallel composite fibers, cortical composite fibers, island composite fibers, and dispersed primary fibers. Parallel composite fibers are aligned in the same direction, providing strength and stiffness. Cortical composite fibers have a thick outer layer for added protection. Island composite fibers have isolated segments of different materials, offering unique properties. Dispersed primary fibers are scattered throughout a matrix, enhancing durability and impact resistance. Each type of composite fiber serves a specific purpose in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
