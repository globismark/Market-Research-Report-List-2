<p><ul><li>Outer Self-dimming Mirror</li><li>Inside Self-dimming Mirror</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive self-dimming mirrors are typically categorized into two main types: Outer self-dimming mirrors and Inside self-dimming mirrors. Outer self-dimming mirrors are designed to reduce glare from the headlights of vehicles behind, enhancing driver visibility and safety. Inside self-dimming mirrors work similarly, but are located inside the vehicle, minimizing distractions for the driver. Both types of self-dimming mirrors use sensors to detect the level of ambient light and automatically adjust the tint of the mirror to reduce glare and improve visibility.</p></p>
