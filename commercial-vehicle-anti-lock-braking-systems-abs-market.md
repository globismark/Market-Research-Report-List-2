<p><p>Commercial Vehicle Anti-lock Braking Systems (ABS) market can be categorized into four types based on the number of channels: Four Channels ABS, Three Channels ABS, Two Channels ABS, and Single Channel ABS. Four Channels ABS involves independent control of all four wheels, ensuring optimal braking performance. Three Channels ABS controls the front wheels together and the rear wheels separately. Two Channels ABS controls the front wheels together and the rear wheels together. Single Channel ABS controls all wheels together, offering the most basic level of anti-lock braking system functionality.</p></p>
