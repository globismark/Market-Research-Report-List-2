<p><p>Fall protection equipment market includes various types of products such as harnesses, lanyards, self-retracting lifelines, belts, and others. Harnesses are worn around the body to distribute fall forces and prevent injury. Lanyards are connecting devices used to secure the harness to an anchor point. Self-retracting lifelines automatically stop the fall by locking the cable in place. Belts provide additional support and stability. Other equipment may include anchor points, connectors, and all necessary accessories for comprehensive fall protection.</p></p>
