<p><p>Strapping machines are available in three main types based on their level of automation: Fully automatic strapping machines, automatic strapping machines, and semi-automatic strapping machines. Fully automatic machines require minimal human intervention and are suitable for high-volume production environments. Automatic machines are partially automated and offer a balance between efficiency and cost. Semi-automatic machines are the most basic type and require manual input for feeding and strapping, making them ideal for smaller businesses or low-volume operations.</p></p>
