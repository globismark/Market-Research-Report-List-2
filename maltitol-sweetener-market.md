<p><p>Maltitol is a sugar alcohol commonly used as a low-calorie sweetener in various food and beverage products. It is often used as a sugar substitute in sugar-free or reduced-sugar products to provide sweetness without the added calories. As consumers become more health-conscious and look for alternatives to sugar, the demand for maltitol sweeteners is expected to increase. Additionally, the growth of the diabetic population and rising awareness about the health risks associated with high sugar consumption are also contributing to the growth of the maltitol sweetener market.</p><p>One of the key trends in the Maltitol Sweetener Market is the increasing use of maltitol in functional food and beverage products.
