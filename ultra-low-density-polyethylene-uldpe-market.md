ULDPE with Melt Mass <1.0 is used in applications like film products, bottles, and injection molding. Melt Mass 1.0~4.0 ULDPE is commonly found in packaging films, protective films, and agricultural films. ULDPE with Melt Mass >4.0 is preferred for applications requiring higher mechanical properties such as geomembranes and industrial films.</p></p>
