<p><p>Retort machines are used for various types of sterilization processes in the food industry. Batch sterilization involves processing a fixed amount of product at a time, making it suitable for smaller-scale operations. Continuous sterilization, on the other hand, allows for a continuous flow of product through the machine, making it more efficient for larger production volumes. Both types of retort machines play a crucial role in ensuring food safety by effectively eliminating harmful microorganisms and extending the shelf life of products.</p></p>
