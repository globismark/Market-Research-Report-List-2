<p><strong>The Hollow Shaft Encoder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incremental Hollow Shaft Encoder</li><li>Absolute Hollow Shaft Encoder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hollow shaft encoders are classified into two main types: Incremental and Absolute. Incremental hollow shaft encoders provide position information by generating pulses as the shaft rotates, allowing for tracking of direction and speed. Absolute hollow shaft encoders, on the other hand, provide a unique digital code for each position, ensuring accurate and reliable position feedback. Both types of encoders are widely used in various industries such as robotics, automation, and manufacturing for precise motion control and positioning applications.</p></p>
