<p><p>Wafer dicing tape is used in the semiconductor industry to hold wafers in place during the dicing process. The market for wafer dicing tape is divided into two main types: double coated and single coated. Double coated tape has adhesive on both sides, providing stronger hold and better stability during dicing. Single coated tape has adhesive on only one side, making it suitable for certain applications where a less aggressive hold is needed. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring precision and efficiency in wafer dicing operations.</p></p>
