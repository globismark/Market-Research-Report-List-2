<p><ul><li>Fluorescent Paint</li><li>Phosphorescent Paint</li><li>Radio Luminescent Paint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Luminous paint market comprises three main types: Fluorescent paint, Phosphorescent paint, and Radio Luminescent paint. Fluorescent paint absorbs ultraviolet light and emits visible light, creating a bright, vibrant glow. Phosphorescent paint stores light energy when exposed to light and continues to emit light in the dark. Radio Luminescent paint uses a radioactive material to emit light without needing exposure to light first. Each type offers unique characteristics and applications, catering to different needs in various industries such as safety and design.</p></p>
