<p><p>The car tachograph market is segmented into integrated and portable types. Integrated tachographs are built directly into the vehicle's dashboard and record driving data continuously. They are commonly used in commercial trucks and buses. Portable tachographs, on the other hand, are detachable devices that can be easily transferred between vehicles.
