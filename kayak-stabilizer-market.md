<p><ul><li>Solid Floatation Devices（Non-inflatable）</li><li>Inflatable Pontoon</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Kayak stabilizers are available in two main types: solid floatation devices (non-inflatable) and inflatable pontoons. Solid floatation devices are made of sturdy materials such as plastic or foam, providing a stable base for the kayak. In contrast, inflatable pontoons are filled with air and can be easily deflated for storage and transportation. Both types of stabilizers help improve the stability and buoyancy of the kayak, making it easier and safer to navigate the water.</p></p>
