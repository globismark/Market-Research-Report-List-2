<p><p>The Automotive Pre-Crash Seatbelt market consists of two main types: Preload Type Belt and Preroll Preload Type Belt. Preload Type Belt is designed to tighten the seatbelt upon impact to secure the passenger in place before a crash occurs. On the other hand, Preroll Preload Type Belt automatically tightens the seatbelt before a crash based on sensors detecting an imminent collision, providing additional protection. Both types aim to enhance occupant safety and reduce the risk of injury in automotive accidents.</p></p>
