<p><p>Dog training machines are available in three main types: sound mode, vibration mode, and electrostatic pulse mode. In sound mode, the machine emits a high-pitched noise to get the dog's attention and correct behavior. Vibration mode uses a gentle vibration to signal to the dog. Electrostatic pulse mode delivers a small electric shock as a deterrent. Each mode offers a different approach to training dogs and can be effective depending on the dog's temperament and training needs.</p></p>
