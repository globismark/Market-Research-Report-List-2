<p><p>LNG filling stations can be classified into two main market types: mobile stations and permanent stations. Mobile stations are equipped with portable LNG tanks and can be relocated to different locations as needed. These are typically used in areas with fluctuating demand for LNG fuel. Permanent stations, on the other hand, are fixed facilities with on-site storage tanks and dispensing equipment. They are established in high-demand locations such as major highways or ports. Both types of stations play a crucial role in meeting the growing demand for LNG fuel in the transportation sector.</p></p>
