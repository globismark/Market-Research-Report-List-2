<p><p>Automotive Cockpit SoCs are classified based on the process technology used in their manufacturing. Under 14 nm Process SoCs are the most advanced, featuring smaller transistors for high performance and energy efficiency. Automotive manufacturers choose the type of SoC based on their specific requirements for processing power, power consumption, and cost.</p></p>
