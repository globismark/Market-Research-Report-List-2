<p><strong>The Fiber Optics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-Mode Fiber Optics</li><li>Single-Mode Fiber Optics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber optics market can be broadly divided into two types - Multi-Mode Fiber Optics and Single-Mode Fiber Optics. Multi-Mode Fiber Optics allow multiple light modes to propagate, making them ideal for shorter distances and high data rates. On the other hand, Single-Mode Fiber Optics enable only one mode of light to travel, suitable for long-distance communications with lower attenuation. Both types cater to different needs and applications in the telecommunications, network infrastructure, and data center industries.</p></p>
