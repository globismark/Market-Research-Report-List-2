<p><p>Marine TV antennas come in two main types: omnidirectional and orientation market. Omnidirectional antennas receive signals from all directions, making them ideal for boats that are constantly moving or changing direction. On the other hand, orientation market antennas are designed to receive signals from specific directions, enabling boaters to pick up signals from a particular location with greater clarity and consistency. Both types of antennas have their own advantages and are suited for different boating environments and needs.</p></p>
