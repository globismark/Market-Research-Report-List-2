<p><p>Pipe fittings and flanges are essential components used in piping systems to connect and secure pipes. Flanges are flat, usually circular discs with evenly spaced holes for bolts, allowing for easy attachment and removal of pipes. On the other hand, pipe fittings are used to change direction, branch off, or terminate a pipe in a plumbing or piping system. Both flanges and pipe fittings come in various types such as weld neck, slip-on, socket weld, threaded, and more to suit different piping requirements.</p></p>
