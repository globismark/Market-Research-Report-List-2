<p><p>A Full Size C-Arm is a medical imaging device used for obtaining real-time X-ray images during surgical procedures. It consists of an X-ray source and detector that rotate around the patient to provide images from multiple angles. Full Size C-Arms are commonly used in orthopedic, cardiovascular, and general surgery procedures.</p><p>The Full Size C-Arm Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.3% during the forecast period. The growth of the market can be attributed to factors such as increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, technological advancements in imaging systems, rising demand for minimally invasive surgeries, and growing geriatric population. Additionally, the increasing number of ambulatory surgical centers and hospitals worldwide is also fueling the demand for Full Size C-Arm systems.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the Full Size C-Arm Market is the integration of advanced features such as 3D imaging, flat-panel detectors, and image-guided systems. These improvements enhance the quality of images, increase efficiency during procedures, and improve patient outcomes. Furthermore, the rising adoption of mobile C-Arm systems for point-of-care imaging is also driving market growth. This trend is particularly prominent in developing countries where access to traditional imaging facilities is limited.</p></p>
