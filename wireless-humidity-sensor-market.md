<p><strong>The Wireless Humidity Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Relative Humidity Sensor</li><li>Absolute Humidity Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wireless Humidity Sensor Market includes two main types: Relative Humidity Sensors and Absolute Humidity Sensors. Relative Humidity Sensors measure the amount of moisture in the air relative to the maximum amount it can hold at a given temperature, providing a percentage value. Absolute Humidity Sensors, on the other hand, measure the exact amount of water vapor present in the air, typically expressed in grams per cubic meter. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in various industries such as agriculture, pharmaceuticals, and HVAC for monitoring and controlling humidity levels.</p></p>
