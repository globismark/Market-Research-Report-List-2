<p><p>Embedded Substrate Technology (ETS) refers to a type of packaging technology used in semiconductor devices. There are three main types of ETS markets: Embedded Passive Substrate (EPS) where passive components are embedded within the substrate, Embedded Trace Substrate (ETS) where trace lines are embedded for interconnections, and Embedded Dies Substrate (EDS) where active dies are embedded for compact and efficient packaging. Each market type serves different purposes in enhancing the performance and functionality of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
