<p><p>SWIR cameras are classified into two main types - SWIR Area Cameras and SWIR Linear Cameras. SWIR Area Cameras capture images in a two-dimensional area format, making them ideal for applications such as machine vision and surveillance. On the other hand, SWIR Linear Cameras capture images in a one-dimensional line format, making them suitable for tasks like spectroscopy and industrial inspection. Both types of cameras offer unique advantages in terms of sensitivity to shortwave infrared light and are widely used in various industries for different applications.</p></p>
