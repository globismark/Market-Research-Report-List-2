<p><strong>The Optical Time Domain Reflectometers OTDR Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bench Top OTDR</li><li>Rack Mount OTDR</li><li>Handheld OTDR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Time Domain Reflectometers (OTDR) are used to measure the characteristics of fiber optic cables by sending short pulses of light down the cable and analyzing the reflections that come back. There are three main types of OTDRs: Bench Top OTDRs, Rack Mount OTDRs, and Handheld OTDRs. Bench Top OTDRs are typically used in lab settings for detailed and precise measurements. Rack Mount OTDRs are designed for integration into larger systems. Handheld OTDRs are portable and widely used for field testing and troubleshooting of fiber optic networks.</p></p>
