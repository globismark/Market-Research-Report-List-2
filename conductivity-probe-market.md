<p><ul><li>Ring Probe</li><li>Pen Probe</li><li>Needle Probe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Conductivity probes are devices used to measure the electrical conductivity of a substance. There are three main types of conductivity probes: Ring Probe, Pen Probe, and Needle Probe. Ring Probes are larger and are typically used for bulk samples, Pen Probes are smaller and more portable for field use, and Needle Probes are fine-tipped for measuring small volumes or for insertion into liquids. Each type of probe has its own unique applications and advantages, catering to different needs in various industries.</p></p>
