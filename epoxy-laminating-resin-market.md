<p><p>Epoxy laminating resin market can be categorized into two main types: Unfilled and Filled. Unfilled resins are pure epoxy resins without any added fillers, making them highly customizable and suitable for various applications. Filled resins, on the other hand, contain added fillers such as silica or glass fibers, enhancing their strength, stiffness, and thermal conductivity. Filled resins are commonly used in applications where mechanical properties are a priority, such as in aerospace and automotive industries. Both types offer unique advantages and are chosen based on specific requirements.</p></p>
