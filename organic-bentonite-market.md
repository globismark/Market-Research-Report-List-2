<p><p>Organic bentonite is classified into three main types based on the cation present in its structure. These include sodium organic bentonite, which is commonly used in drilling fluids and foundry applications due to its high swelling capacity. Calcium organic bentonite is preferred in environmental protection applications due to its good gelation properties. The "Others" category comprises other types of cations present in the organic bentonite structure, such as magnesium or potassium, which may have specific uses in various industries.</p></p>
