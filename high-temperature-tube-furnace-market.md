<p><ul><li>Horizontal</li><li>Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High temperature tube furnaces are classified based on their orientation as horizontal and vertical markets. Horizontal tube furnaces are designed for applications that require uniform heating across a sample, making them ideal for experiments such as annealing, sintering, and heat treating. Vertical tube furnaces, on the other hand, are commonly used for applications that require gravity-fed processes, such as chemical vapor deposition and crystal growth. Both types of tube furnaces have specific advantages and are chosen based on the requirements of the experiment or process.</p></p>
