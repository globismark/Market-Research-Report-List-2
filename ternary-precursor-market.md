<p><p>Ternary Precursor Markets consist of NCM Type and NCA Type variants. NCM Type is characterized by a combination of nickel, cobalt, and manganese, offering high energy density suitable for electric vehicle batteries. On the other hand, NCA Type includes nickel, cobalt, and aluminum, known for its high energy density and stability. Both types are widely used in the production of lithium-ion batteries, with NCM Type being more common in consumer electronics, while NCA Type is often preferred for electric vehicles.</p></p>
