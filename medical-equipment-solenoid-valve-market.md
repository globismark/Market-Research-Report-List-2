<p><p>The Medical Equipment Solenoid Valve Market consists of two main types: Direct Acting and Pilot Operated. </p><p>Direct Acting solenoid valves operate directly on the main valve and do not require an external power source, making them simpler and more cost-effective. </p><p>Pilot Operated solenoid valves use a small pilot valve to control the flow of the main valve, allowing for greater precision and control. They are commonly used in applications where high flow rates or pressure differentials are required.</p></p>
