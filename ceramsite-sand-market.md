<p><ul><li>Gravel Shaped</li><li>Spherical</li><li>Cylindrical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramsite sand is classified into three main market types based on their shape: gravel-shaped, spherical, and cylindrical. Gravel-shaped ceramsite sand is irregularly shaped and commonly used for landscaping and construction purposes. Spherical ceramsite sand is round in shape, providing better flowability and resistance to crushing, making it suitable for use in lightweight concrete. Cylindrical ceramsite sand is elongated in shape and often used for filters and drainage systems due to its high porosity and water permeability. Each market type serves different applications based on their unique characteristics.</p></p>
