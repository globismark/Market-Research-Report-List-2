<p><p>LED encapsulants are used to protect LEDs from external factors such as moisture, dust, and mechanical stress. Three common types of encapsulants are epoxides, polyimides, and silicones. Epoxides offer high mechanical strength and thermal stability. Polyimides provide excellent electrical insulation and chemical resistance. Silicones, including RTV and gel forms, offer flexibility, water resistance, and ease of application. Each type of encapsulant has unique properties that cater to different LED applications, ensuring reliable performance and durability.</p></p>
