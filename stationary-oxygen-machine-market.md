<p><p>Stationary Oxygen Machine market can be classified into various types such as Molecular Sieve, Macromolecule Permeable Membrane, Electrolysis, Chemical, and Others. Molecular Sieve technology utilizes a porous material to separate oxygen from other gases. Macromolecule Permeable Membrane technology relies on a selective membrane to filter oxygen. Electrolysis technology splits water molecules to produce oxygen. Chemical technology involves the use of chemicals to release oxygen. Other types may include advanced or alternative technologies for oxygen production in stationary machines.</p></p>
