<p><p>Pulsed radar systems are classified based on the frequency band they operate in. The L Band radar market operates at frequencies between 1-2 GHz and is commonly used for air traffic control and weather monitoring. The X Band radar market operates at frequencies between 8-12 GHz and is utilized for military and maritime applications. The C Band radar market operates at frequencies between 4-8 GHz and is commonly used for long-range weather surveillance. The S Band radar market operates at frequencies between 2-4 GHz and is used for various applications including air traffic control and defense systems.</p></p>
