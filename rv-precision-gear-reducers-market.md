<p><p>RV Precision Gear Reducers are available in two main types - Flange Output Type and Hollow Type. Flange Output Type gear reducers have a solid output shaft with a mounting flange, making them suitable for applications where a solid connection is required. On the other hand, Hollow Type gear reducers have a hollow output shaft, allowing for a wider range of applications where a through shaft or hollow bore is necessary. Both types offer high precision and efficiency, making them popular choices in various industries.</p></p>
