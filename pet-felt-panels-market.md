<p><ul><li>Below 7 mm</li><li>7-10 mm</li><li>10-15 mm</li><li>15-25 mm</li><li>Above 25 mm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PET felt panels come in various thicknesses to cater to different needs in the market. Below 7 mm panels are lightweight and suitable for decorative purposes. 7-10 mm panels offer a balance of weight and durability, making them ideal for soundproofing and insulation. 10-15 mm panels provide increased sound absorption and structural support. 15-25 mm panels are preferred for heavy-duty applications requiring high sound absorption. Above 25 mm panels are the thickest and offer maximum soundproofing capabilities, commonly used in commercial and industrial settings.</p></p>
