<p><strong>The Automotive Hydraulic Brake Booster Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Booster Body</li><li>Booster Piston</li><li>Piston Return Spring</li><li>Reaction Mechanism</li><li>Control Valve Mechanism</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Hydraulic Brake Booster market includes various components such as Booster Body, Booster Piston, Piston Return Spring, Reaction Mechanism, and Control Valve Mechanism. The Booster Body houses the booster piston, which is responsible for increasing brake force. The Piston Return Spring ensures the piston returns to its original position after braking. The Reaction Mechanism translates the force applied on the brake pedal to the booster piston. The Control Valve Mechanism regulates the flow of hydraulic fluid to control braking pressure.</p></p>
