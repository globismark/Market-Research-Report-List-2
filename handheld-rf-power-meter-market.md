<p><p>Handheld RF power meters are available in two main types: analog and digital. Analog power meters provide real-time readings in a continuous analog format, making them ideal for tasks that require quick visual assessment of RF power levels. On the other hand, digital power meters display readings in a numerical format, offering more precise and accurate measurements. Digital power meters also often come with additional features such as data logging and connectivity options. Both analog and digital power meters cater to different user preferences and application requirements in the market.</p></p>
