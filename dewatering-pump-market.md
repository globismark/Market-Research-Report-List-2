<p><p>The dewatering pump market consists of two main types: submersible dewatering pumps and non-submersible dewatering pumps. Submersible dewatering pumps are designed to be fully submerged in water, making them suitable for applications where water needs to be drained or removed from a specific area. Non-submersible dewatering pumps, on the other hand, are designed to operate outside of the water, typically used for pumping water from wells or other sources. Each type of pump has its own set of advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the dewatering application.</p></p>
