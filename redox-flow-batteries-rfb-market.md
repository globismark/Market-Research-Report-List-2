<p><p>Redox Flow Batteries (RFB) are a type of rechargeable battery that store and release energy through redox reactions. There are two main types of RFBs in the market - Vanadium Redox Flow Battery and Hybrid Flow Battery. Vanadium RFBs use vanadium electrolytes to store energy, allowing for long cycle life and high efficiency. Hybrid RFBs combine features of different types of flow batteries to optimize performance and cost-effectiveness. Both types of RFBs are gaining popularity for grid energy storage and renewable energy integration.</p></p>
