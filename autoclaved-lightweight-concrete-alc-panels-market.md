<p><p>Autoclaved Lightweight Concrete (ALC) Panels are divided into two market types based on their raw materials: Silica Sand and Fly Ash. Silica Sand ALC panels are made from a mix of cement, silica sand, lime, and aluminum powder, resulting in a strong and durable product. Fly Ash ALC panels use fly ash, a byproduct of coal combustion, which helps reduce environmental impact. Both types of ALC panels are popular choices for construction due to their lightweight nature, high insulation properties, and fire resistance.</p></p>
