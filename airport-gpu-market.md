<p><p>The Airport GPU market consists of two main types: Mobile GPU and Fixed GPU. Mobile GPUs are portable units that can be easily moved around the airport to provide power to aircraft parked at various locations. Fixed GPUs, on the other hand, are permanently installed at specific locations on the airport apron to provide consistent power supply to aircraft. Both types of GPUs play a crucial role in ensuring smooth operations and efficiency in aircraft ground handling processes at airports.</p></p>
