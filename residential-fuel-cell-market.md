<p><strong>The Residential Fuel Cell Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PEM</li><li>SOFC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are two main types of residential fuel cells on the market: PEM (Proton Exchange Membrane) and SOFC (Solid Oxide Fuel Cell). PEM fuel cells use a polymer electrolyte membrane, while SOFC fuel cells use a solid oxide electrolyte. PEM fuel cells are more compact and operate at lower temperatures, making them suitable for residential use. On the other hand, SOFC fuel cells are known for their high efficiency and ability to use a variety of fuels, but they are typically larger and more expensive.</p></p>
