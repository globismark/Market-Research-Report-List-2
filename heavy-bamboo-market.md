<p><p>Heavy bamboo can be manufactured using two main market types: cold pressing and hot pressing. In cold pressing, the bamboo is compressed at room temperature using high pressure to create strong and durable products. On the other hand, hot pressing involves heating the bamboo before applying pressure, resulting in a smoother and more polished finish. Both market types are popular choices for creating various bamboo products such as flooring, furniture, and decor items due to their strength and versatility.</p></p>
