<p><p>Low and Medium Voltage Switchgear market includes two main types: Gas Insulated Switchgear (GIS) and Air Insulated Switchgear (AIS). GIS utilizes sulfur hexafluoride gas to insulate and protect the electrical components, providing a smaller footprint and higher reliability. AIS uses air to insulate the components and is more cost-effective but requires larger indoor or outdoor space. Both types offer solutions for controlling, protecting, and isolating electrical equipment in power distribution networks while catering to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
