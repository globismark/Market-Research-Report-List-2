<p><p>Mobile control valves are classified into two main types: monoblock valves and sectional valves. Monoblock valves are compact, one-piece units that are easy to install and maintain. They are commonly used in smaller hydraulic systems. On the other hand, sectional valves are modular in design and allow for customization based on the specific requirements of the hydraulic system. They are preferred for larger and more complex hydraulic systems where flexibility and control are crucial.</p></p>
