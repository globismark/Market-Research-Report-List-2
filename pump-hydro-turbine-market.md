<p><p>A Pump Hydro Turbine is a type of hydroelectric power generation system that functions by using two water reservoirs at different elevations to generate electricity. During times of low electricity demand, excess power from the grid is used to pump water from the lower reservoir to the upper reservoir. When electricity demand is high, water is released from the upper reservoir to flow through turbines, generating power.</p><p>The Pump Hydro Turbine Market is expected to experience significant growth at a CAGR of 8% during the forecast period.
