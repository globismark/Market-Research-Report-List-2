<p><p>Copy routers are machines used for woodworking, specifically for duplicating shapes and patterns. There are three main types of copy routers based on the number of spindles they have: 1-spindle, 2-spindle, and 3-spindle. 1-spindle copy routers are best for smaller scale projects that require less precision. 2-spindle copy routers are ideal for medium-sized projects that need more intricacy. 3-spindle copy routers are used for large-scale projects with high precision requirements. Each type of copy router caters to different needs of woodworking professionals.</p></p>
