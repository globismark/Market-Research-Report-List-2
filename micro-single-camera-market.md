<p><ul><li>Mirrorless System</li><li>Fixed Mirror System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro-single camera market can be categorized into two main types: mirrorless system and fixed mirror system. Mirrorless system cameras do not have the traditional mirror mechanism found in DSLRs, resulting in a more compact and lightweight design. On the other hand, fixed mirror system cameras utilize a fixed mirror to reflect light onto the sensor, offering a middle ground between traditional DSLRs and mirrorless cameras. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of photographers, providing a diverse range of options in the market.</p></p>
