<p><p>Conductive fibers market is segmented into types such as Conductive Polyester, Conductive Polyamine, and Others. Conductive Polyester fibers are made by blending conductive materials with traditional polyester fibers, providing good conductivity and flexibility. Conductive Polyamine fibers are produced by incorporating conductive additives into polyamine polymers, offering excellent thermal and electrical conductivity. The "Others" segment includes conductive fibers made from materials like metal and carbon, which are utilized in various applications such as smart textiles, EMI shielding, and sensors.</p></p>
