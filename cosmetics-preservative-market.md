<p><p>Cosmetics preservatives can be categorized into different types based on their chemical composition and mode of action. Phenol derivatives, such as parabens, are commonly used as preservatives due to their antimicrobial properties. Formaldehyde-releasing preservatives are effective in preventing bacterial and fungal growth in cosmetic products. Organic acids, such as sorbic acid and benzoic acid, are also widely used as preservatives. Other types of preservatives may include natural ingredients, such as plant extracts, or newer synthetic compounds developed for specific applications in the cosmetics industry.</p></p>
